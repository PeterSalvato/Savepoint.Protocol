Savepoint Protocol

Version Control for Thought

Reasoning in progress is the most perishable thing in a knowledge workflow. Not files, not decisions — the thinking-path that connects them. Context windows close. It disappears.

Savepoint is a protocol for marking the exact moment a thinking-path forks — before the context closes. Three documents: conventions.md, symbol-index.md, and standardized context prompts. Governance the AI cannot rewrite.

Open source. v3.0. Works on paper, in plaintext, or in a CLI.
